# Bahasa Inggris — Kelas 6 (Kurikulum Merdeka)

## Capaian Pembelajaran
- Learners understand short oral and written texts about experiences, plans, opinions, public places, and simple information reports.
- Learners express needs, suggestions, comparisons, and preferences using familiar vocabulary and clear sentence patterns.
- Learners read narrative, descriptive, procedural, and functional texts to identify sequence, purpose, details, and implicit meaning.
- Learners write short guided texts with connected sentences, appropriate conjunctions, and basic grammar accuracy.

## Bab 1: My Past Experiences
**Topik utama:** Recount text, past activities, and sequence words.

**Sub-konsep:** Simple past verbs, regular and common irregular verbs, yesterday/last week/last holiday, first-next-then-finally, feelings after an event, details in a personal recount.

**Sample teks bacaan:** Last Sunday, my family visited a small museum. First, we looked at old photographs. Then, a guide explained a traditional musical instrument. Finally, I wrote notes in my notebook. I felt happy because I learned many new things.

**Kosakata kunci:** visited, looked, explained, learned, museum, guide, photograph, instrument, first, next, then, finally, happy, excited.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Identify past-time expressions, sequence events in a recount, choose correct past verbs, and answer detail or main idea questions from a short recount text.

## Bab 2: Directions and Public Places
**Topik utama:** Giving directions, public places, and map-based language.

**Sub-konsep:** Library, hospital, post office, market, bus stop, park; turn left/right, go straight, across from, next to, between; asking for and giving directions politely.

**Sample teks bacaan:** The library is next to the park. From the school gate, go straight and turn left at the traffic light. The library is across from the post office. It opens at eight o'clock in the morning.

**Kosakata kunci:** library, hospital, market, post office, bus stop, park, traffic light, go straight, turn left, turn right, across from, next to.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Understand route instructions, identify public places from clues, choose suitable prepositions of place, and complete polite direction dialogues.

## Bab 3: Stories and Moral Lessons
**Topik utama:** Narrative text, characters, setting, problem, solution, and moral lesson.

**Sub-konsep:** Fairy tales and fables, character traits, time and place setting, conflict, resolution, moral values, dialogue markers, adjectives for personality.

**Sample teks bacaan:** A small turtle wanted to join a race. The rabbit laughed because the turtle walked slowly. During the race, the rabbit slept under a tree. The turtle kept walking and won. The story teaches us to be patient and never give up.

**Kosakata kunci:** story, character, setting, problem, solution, moral lesson, turtle, rabbit, slowly, race, patient, never give up, kind, lazy.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Identify narrative elements, infer moral lessons, describe character traits, and answer questions about sequence and cause-effect in a story.

## Bab 4: Health and Safety
**Topik utama:** Health problems, advice, rules, and safety signs.

**Sub-konsep:** Fever, cough, stomachache, headache, toothache; should/should not; must/must not; washing hands, healthy habits, medicine labels, warning signs, emergency instructions.

**Sample teks bacaan:** Lani has a cough and a fever. She should drink warm water and rest at home. She should not share her bottle with friends. At school, students must wash their hands before eating to keep everyone healthy.

**Kosakata kunci:** fever, cough, headache, stomachache, toothache, rest, medicine, warm water, should, should not, must, must not, safety, healthy.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Match health problems with advice, interpret simple safety signs, choose correct modal verbs, and understand rules for healthy behavior.

## Bab 5: Future Plans and Invitations
**Topik utama:** Plans, invitations, accepting and declining politely.

**Sub-konsep:** Going to, want to, would like to, invitation phrases, days and dates, places for activities, reasons for accepting or declining, short message format.

**Sample teks bacaan:** Dear Maya, I am going to have a study group on Friday afternoon. We will meet at my house at three o'clock. Would you like to join us? Please bring your English notebook. From, Niko.

**Kosakata kunci:** plan, invitation, study group, join, bring, Friday, afternoon, would like to, accept, decline, sorry, maybe next time.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Identify purpose and details of an invitation, choose polite responses, complete future-plan sentences, and infer time/place information from a short message.

## Bab 6: Simple Reports about Nature and Technology
**Topik utama:** Information report text about animals, natural objects, and simple technology.

**Sub-konsep:** General classification, description, facts, habitat, function, parts of an object, comparison, cause-effect connectors because/so, using present tense for facts.

**Sample teks bacaan:** A solar lamp uses sunlight as energy. During the day, the panel collects sunlight. At night, the lamp can shine without using electricity from the wall. Solar lamps are useful in places that get a lot of sunshine.

**Kosakata kunci:** report, fact, solar lamp, sunlight, energy, panel, useful, collect, shine, nature, technology, because, so, function.

**Kompetensi yang diuji:** Find factual details in an information report, identify purpose and function, choose because/so in cause-effect sentences, and distinguish fact from opinion in simple English.
